Description

Onmego is a hybrid mobile application designed for radio stations to increase retention rate, frequency of use, and revenue. It allows radio stations to capture and retain more users by giving a them a user friendly app to stream their content.

By utilizing geo fencing, we are able to serve targeted advertisements to users based on their location. After the app "learns" what kind of content the user likes, we can serve them ever further targeted advertisements.
