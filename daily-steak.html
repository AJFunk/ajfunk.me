Description

Daily Steak is a mobile app that gives users the ability to get multiple perspectives on the same news story. Our "Pandora for news" app learns whay you like/dislike, and personalizes your news feed. Stories are read out loud to you, and you can use voice commands to skip/rewind/etc.

The app also allows users to "dig in" to a story, which would then read the news stories from different new sources about the same story. This allows users to compare different perspectives.
